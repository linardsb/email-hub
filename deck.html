The Problem

Email development is fragmented.

Fragmented Tooling

Teams stitch together separate editors, preview services, QA checkers, ESP dashboards, and approval threads for every single campaign.

Slow, Repetitive Builds

Every campaign re-solves the same rendering and fallback problems by hand. Iteration drags out across days.

Knowledge That Evaporates

A fix discovered on one client is invisible on the next. Expertise lives in people’s heads, not in the system.

For an agency serving many brands across different platforms, this friction compounds with every engagement.

The Idea

One platform. Every stage.

From brief to inbox — write, preview, test, approve, and export without ever leaving the platform.

Proven Innovation

The Landscape

Most tools

Built for in-house brand teams running a single email programme on a single platform. AI bolted on for content generation.

The Hub

Built for the multi-client agency model — compounding knowledge across clients, and the freedom to deploy to any platform.
